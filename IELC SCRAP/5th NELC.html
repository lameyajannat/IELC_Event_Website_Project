5th IELC NELC

In the year 2024 with much anticipation. IELC made a grand return with the 5th National English Language Carnival on May 10 and 11, featuring a rich variety of events that captured the attention of thousands of students nationwide and provided them with a platform to showcase their inner talents and potentials.

Following the early announcement of the festival, the student body eagerly registered, and participation was very quick. The 5th National English Language Carnival (NELC) witnessed an impressive turnout of over 3,500 competitors from 125 institutions, each striving to claim their prize. The festival's prestige has been solidified through the unwavering dedication of the Moderator and Co-Patron, securing its place in the annals of national acclaim for both NELC and Ideal School and College.

Over the course of its two-day duration, the event featured a succession of esteemed guests. On the inaugural day, the spotlight was on Chief Guest Touhid Uddin Afridi. The following day, we were honored by the presence of Robiul Islam and Alamin Sarkar. We extend our deepest appreciation to Eajaj Mahamud Arick, President and Mahathir Ann Nafi, General Secretary, as well as the entire 7th Executive Committee, whose exceptional commitment and tireless efforts were pivotal to the realization of the 5th NELC.

In this very festival, we presented a total of 14 exciting events across different categories. There were linguistic events such as the English Olympiad, Wordgame, Instant Story Writing and Spell Master. Speaking-based events included Turn Coat, Extempore Speech & Quizzing-based events like Team-Based Quiz and Marvel & DC Quiz. Presentation and Submission-based events featured Wall Magazine, Multimedia Presentation, Scrapbook, Composition and Sketch Submission and Photography. However, the highlight was the signature event 'Spot the Fallacy'.

Reflecting on the 5th NELC, we extend our deepest gratitude to all participants, volunteers, coordinators, sponsors, and organizers. Their collective efforts have rendered this festival one of the most triumphant in the annals of IELC history. We take great pride in acknowledging our esteemed sponsors: Eastern Bank PLC, our title sponsor; Bangladesh Commerce Bank, our powering partner; Pioneer Denim, our co-powering partner; and our esteemed associations with The Daily Star, MMS Global Services, and the Thalassemia Foundation Hospital.

Additionally, we are grateful to Sondiper Nir for their role as our food partner, Jibon for hydration, Somoy TV as our media partner, and Tickhost for ticketing, all of whom have been instrumental in bringing the 5th NELC to fruition.

The two-day event left an unforgettable mark on all who participated. The carnival was far more than just a competition; it served as a dynamic platform where like-minded individuals from across the country united to celebrate their passion for linguistic and creative expression. The carnival's success highlighted the exceptional dedication and tireless efforts of the 7th Executive Committee and others who contributed. We look forward to many more years of celebrating talent, encouraging growth, and building a community of passionate learners. Let's move forward by embracing this spirit, establishing a lasting legacy of learning, growth, and creativity.
