Eloquence 3.0

Eloquence 3.0, an intra-club event, was held on Monday, September 16, 2024, at the Banasree branch of Ideal School and College. Hosting the event at the Banasree branch, rather than the main branch, highlights the club's ability to organize successful events at any venue without interruption.

Since its founding, the Ideal English Language Club has hosted a variety of engaging events designed to highlight the skills and talents of students in the international language of English. One of the club's most celebrated initiatives, "Eloquence," was created to help students unlock their full potential. Over the years, "Eloquence" has become a well-known event organized by the club, recognized for providing both a learning platform and a space for students to showcase their abilities while opening doors to limitless opportunities. The most recent edition, "Eloquence 3.0," attracted significant attention from English enthusiasts and participants alike, offering them the chance to showcase their immense talent.

"Eloquence 3.0" would not have been possible without the hard work and dedication of many people. We extend our deepest thanks to our moderator, Lovely Akhter, and all the co-moderators for their support. A special thank you goes to the 8th Executive Committee for dedicating their time and efforts to ensure the event's success. We are also grateful to Ahmad Abid Ahsan, the President; Mohammad Bin Wasy, the General Secretary; and all the other executives of the 8th Executive Committee for making this unforgettable event possible through their tireless commitment.

The event featured four exciting competitions. Students from Classes 6 to 10 were divided into two categories:
(1) Junior (classes 6–8)
(2) Senior (classes 9–10)
Over 400 students attended, each showcasing their talents in hopes of earning a prize. The competitions included the English Olympiad, Team-Based Quiz, Instant Story Writing, and Extempore Speech.

Eloquence 3.0 being the recent addition to the illustrious history of Ideal English Language Club, has a unique place in in our hearts. The event's success highlighted the exceptional dedication and tireless efforts of the 8th Executive Committee and the others who contributed. Eloquence 3.0 was far more than just an event, where like-minded people celebrated their passion for linguistic and creative expression.
