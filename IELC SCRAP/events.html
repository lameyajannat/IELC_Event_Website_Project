A legacy of learning:
IELC's Journey through events

  • The Event Journey

Ever since its establishment almost nine years ago, the Ideal English Language Club has successfully organized the National English Language Carnival on five different occasions. The club has also hosted a variety of engaging events, such as Eloquence and Opulence, designed to highlight students' skills and talents in the English language. In addition, IELC has dedicated itself to fostering language proficiency by conducting 17 impactful workshops.
