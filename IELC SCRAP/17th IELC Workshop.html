17th IELC Workshop

The 17th IELC Workshop was a truly memorable gathering, bringing together students, educators, and special guests under the inspiring theme, "Mastering English: A Pathway to Higher Education in a Flourishing Bangladesh." The workshop held in 31 January,2025 was all about opening minds to new possibilities and encouraging students to see English as a bridge to their dreams.

The highlight of the event was an engaging speech by our esteemed guest speaker, who shared personal stories and practical advice on how mastering English can open doors to higher education and future success in Bangladesh. The speaker's insights resonated with everyone, inspiring students to think more deeply about their own goals. This inspiration carried into the lively question-and-answer session that followed, where students eagerly asked questions about language learning and overcoming academic challenges. The guest speaker responded with warmth and encouragement, making each student feel valued and supported.

As the workshop drew to a close, there was a genuine sense of accomplishment and unity in the air. Students, the guest speaker, and the dedicated executives who organized the event gathered for group photos, sharing smiles and laughter. These photos captured the spirit of collaboration, friendship, and shared ambition that defined the day. The atmosphere was filled with pride and optimism, and these moments of connection and celebration will be cherished by everyone involved.
